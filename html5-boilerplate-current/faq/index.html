Do you have outdoor seating available?

Yes, NYC Brunch Club offers outdoor seating to enjoy your meal with a view of the vibrant West Village streets. Our outdoor seating is available on a first-come, first-served basis, weather permitting.

Can I customize my brunch menu for a special occasion?

Absolutely! We offer customizable brunch menus to cater to your special events, ensuring your celebration is perfect and tailored to your preferences.

What is your policy on cancellations and refunds?

We understand that plans can change. For a full refund, please cancel your reservation at least 24 hours in advance; cancellations made within 24 hours are subject to a fee.

Do you offer bottomless drink options for brunch?

Yes, we offer bottomless mimosa and Bloody Mary options during brunch hours. Enjoy unlimited refills with the purchase of any brunch entree.
